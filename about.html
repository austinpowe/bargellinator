Bargellinator is a generative pattern designer.

It randomly creates a never-before-seen Bargello tapestry each time you load the page.

Bargello is an embroidery technique that became popular in the mid-70s but fell into unpopularity in the succeeding decades. It uses brightly colored yarn on canvas or plastic mesh to create repeating patterns.

Because of Bargello's waning popularity, there simply isn't as many patterns available to the modern stitcher as there are for other fiber arts, like knitting, cross-stitch, embroidery, or weaving.

This site my personal attempt to decode what makes Bargello patterns so fun and unique, while hopefully providing inspiration to other stitchers.
